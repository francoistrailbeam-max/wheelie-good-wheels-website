Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Who We Are

Wheelie Good Wheels LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a peer-to-peer car rental business based in Florida, operating through the Turo platform. Our website is wheeliegoodrentals.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit our vehicle co-host inquiry form, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Vehicle details — year, make, and model of the vehicle you'd like to co-host

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to contact you about the Wheelie Good Wheels vehicle co-host program. We will reach out to discuss whether your vehicle is a good fit, explain how the program works, and answer any questions you may have.

We do not use your information for any other purpose without your consent.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for their marketing or commercial purposes. Your information stays with Wheelie Good Wheels LLC, period.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to follow up on your co-host inquiry. If you ask us to delete your information at any time, we will honor that request promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Withdraw from the co-host inquiry process at any time

To exercise any of these rights, just reach out to us directly at Maximus@wheeliegoodrentals.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? We're happy to help.
