Our Story

Wheelie Good Wheels LLC was founded with a simple idea: renting a car shouldn't be a headache. No long lines, no confusing fees, no impersonal service — just a clean, reliable vehicle ready when you need it.

We operate through Turo, the world's largest peer-to-peer car sharing platform, which means you get the convenience of booking online with the personal touch of working directly with real people who care about your experience.

We're a growing fleet and a growing business — and we're proud to be building something that puts customers first at every step.
