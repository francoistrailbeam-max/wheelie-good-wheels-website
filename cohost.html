Co-Host Opportunities

Your car. Our expertise. Real passive income — with zero hassle.

Turn Your Idle Vehicle Into Monthly Income

If you own a vehicle that's sitting in your driveway more than it's on the road, we want to talk. Wheelie Good Wheels manages the entire rental process on your behalf — and you walk away with a check every month.

💰

Earn 70% of Every Trip

You keep the majority of every rental. We handle the bookings, guest communication, and all the logistics. Your car earns while you don't lift a finger.

🛡️

Drop Your Insurance Bill

We provide full commercial coverage — both on-trip and off-trip. Many of our co-hosts are able to eliminate their personal auto insurance entirely, putting even more money back in their pocket.

🔑

Fully Managed, Start to Finish

We take full possession of your vehicle and manage everything — guest vetting, airport pickup and drop-off, cleaning, maintenance coordination, and daily care. You stay completely hands-off.

🏠

Your Car Is Protected

When your vehicle isn't on a trip, it's safely garaged — not sitting exposed on the street. We treat every car in our fleet like it's our own.
