Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Who We Are

Wheelie Good Wheels LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a peer-to-peer car rental business based in Brandon, Florida, operating through the Turo platform. Our website is wheeliegoodrentals.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted via our contact form
  • Messages or inquiries you send us
  • SMS opt-in consent when you check the opt-in box on our contact form

We may also collect limited technical data (browser type, pages visited) through standard web analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and customer service requests
  • Send SMS notifications related to your reservation (booking confirmations, pickup/dropoff reminders) if you have opted in
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. SMS Messaging

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from us, you agree to receive text messages regarding your rental reservation, pickup and dropoff reminders, and customer service updates from Wheelie Good Wheels LLC at the phone number you provided.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Turo — as required to facilitate your rental booking
  • Service providers — such as SMS delivery services (Twilio), solely to deliver our communications to you
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect our rights

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

To exercise these rights, contact us at Maximus@wheeliegoodrentals.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
